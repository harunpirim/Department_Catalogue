Industrial & Manufacturing Systems Engineering (IMSE)

A department catalogue designed to showcase how IMSE blends people, data, and technology to improve systems in healthcare, manufacturing, energy, logistics, and public policy.

Section 1

Why Choose IMSE at NDSU?

IMSE at NDSU prepares students to design, improve, and lead complex systems across healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, technology, and energy. The program emphasizes practical impact through internships, applied projects, and industry collaboration.

What Makes It Distinctive
  • Big-picture systems thinking with data-driven decisions
  • Hands-on learning with modern engineering tools
  • High-demand career paths across multiple industries
  • Strong mentorship from faculty and advisors
